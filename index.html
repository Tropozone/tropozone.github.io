Hybrid. Decentralized. Co-evolving. Polyphonic.

A response to the ecological and social crises facing us, the assemblage exposes us to weird semanitcs, guides us to our relational realities, and brings trans-border and trans-species frictions into our living spaces. Taking the form of a network with geographically distributed nodes, its material presence is scattered across different parts of the geosphere, just like its creators. Composed of humans and nonhumans, the assemblage constantly evolves from exchanges that happen between and across its diverse actors.
